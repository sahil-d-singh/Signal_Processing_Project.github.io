EGT Bracket Fatigue Failure — DSP + ANSYS
Diagnosed exhaust gas temperature sensor bracket cracking using PSD road excitation analysis correlated with ANSYS modal FEA. Resonance detuning strategy predicted 6× fatigue life improvement.

Diagnose → Model → Fix
Diagnose
Welch PSD of road accelerometer data identified peak excitation at 100–250 Hz on cobblestone proving ground. Bracket FRF peak at 180 Hz confirmed resonance coincidence — Q=16.7× amplification (ζ=0.03)
Model
ANSYS modal analysis (material props at 600°C: E –15%, yield –40%). Harmonic response confirmed stress concentration at bracket root matches field crack location. Dirlik rainflow + Miner's rule → 45,000 km predicted life
Fixed
Wall thickness +0.8mm + gusset at root → first mode detuned 180 → 380 Hz (outside road excitation band). Predicted life: 45,000 → 280,000 km. Alternatively: 321 SS material change or rubber-isolated mount
